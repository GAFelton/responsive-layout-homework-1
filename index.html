About Me

I am a web developer from Seattle, WA with entrepreneurship & leadership experience. After earning my MS in Entrepreneurship from UW's Foster School of Business, I started a company with a friend of mine called Lonely Produce. Our goal was to reduce food waste by helping small-medium sized farmers sell their excess goods to institutional buyers. After leaving Lonely Produce, I backpacked through Europe and Africa for 5 months before returning to the states and embarking down this path of skill-building. I earned a bachelors degree in Classics from Whitman College in 2015. I love traveling, playing games, solving problems, and cooking, and I am a fiend for hot sauce.
